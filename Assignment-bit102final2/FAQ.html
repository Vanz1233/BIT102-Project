Vanz's regularly hosts online auctions. A live auction takes place at a specific date and time in one of our salerooms. During a live auction, the auctioneer accepts online bids via vanz's LIVE platform™ and absentee bids to determine the final hammer price and buyer of each lot. An online auction is open for bidding over the course of several days. You can bid online either by placing a one-time quick bid.The countdown timer on each lot indicates how much time is left before the lot closes and the winning bidder is determined.
You cannot cancel a bid once it has been submitted. Each bid you place enters you into a binding contract with Vanz’s.

Selling

Interested sellers can contact us to start the valuation process. Initially, you will be directed to one of our specialists who will guide you through the necessary steps. Before being assigned to a watch specialist, please submit up to five images and provide additional information for each item. If you wish to discuss or clarify any details about your items, you can schedule an appointment to visit our office in Malaysia and meet with our experts in person.
Vanz charges a single Seller’s Commission rate for our services. This commission is a fixed percentage of the final hammer price at auction and includes marketing costs and insurance coverage (excluding Wine sales, where these costs are charged separately). If your item sells for more than the agreed high estimate, an additional 2% Performance Commission fee will apply. You may also incur charges for external services such as shipping, restoration, and framing, which will be discussed and agreed upon with you in advance. VAT (value-added tax) or other applicable duties or taxes may be applied to these fees, depending on the auction site's jurisdiction.

Private Auctions

Vanz's Private Sales service facilitates the buying and selling of classic and rare watches outside the auction room. Leveraging our global network of specialists, we connect buyers and sellers to broker exclusive sales. This discreet, bespoke service offers exceptional flexibility — whether the regular auction schedule doesn't align with your needs, or you have specific items on your collection wishlist that you’d like us to source for you.
