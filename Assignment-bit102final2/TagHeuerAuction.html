Sales Overview

We are thrilled to share the news of our next auction, which will include an amazing collection of vintage timepieces from the most well-known luxury brands in the world.

A vibrant event, a Tag Heuer watch auction features a range of timepieces from the renowned Swiss luxury watchmaker on display for prospective buyers. A wide variety of models are frequently offered at these auctions, ranging from vintage and special edition items to iconic collections like the Monaco and Carrera. Competing bids are made by participants over the phone, online, or in person, with the highest bid winning the item. Watch aficionados, collectors, and investors are drawn to these auctions because they offer a chance to purchase fine, precisely manufactured timepieces that frequently increase in value. These auctions are much anticipated in the watch community because of the thrill of the bidding process and the attraction of owning a Tag Heuer.
