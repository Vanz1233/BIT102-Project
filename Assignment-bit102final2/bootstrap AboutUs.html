About Us

Founded in 2024, Vanz is a rising and opulent auction business specializing in collecting luxury watches across the generations. Vanz expansive connections with the watch specialist offers our clients a comprehensive range of global services including our watch appraisal, reparation and maintenance, warranty and advisory allowing customers to get what they need in our place. We are also committed to ethical practices and responsible culture.

Mission

To appreciate and honor the craftsmanship of watchmakers through the century, bridging admirers with ethereal timepieces and treasure the art of horology.

Vision

To create a world-leading luxury-watch auction experience by integrating avant-garde elements and technology with profound appreciation for classical timepieces, ensuring an unprecedented experience for everyone.
